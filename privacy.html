Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Know Ball ("we," "our," or "us"), a mobile application developed by T.C.. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our game and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, maintain, and improve our app. The data we collect falls into two categories:

  • Information You Provide to Us: If you choose to create an account, contact our support team, or participate in leaderboards, we may collect your email address, chosen username, and support message history.
  • Information Collected Automatically: When you access the app, we automatically collect certain telemetry and usage data. This includes your device type, operating system, unique device identifiers (such as Apple IDFA or Google Advertising ID), IP address, app crash reports, and basic gameplay statistics (such as high scores or progression).

3. Third-Party Services

To operate, monetize, and analyze the app, we utilize trusted third-party services. These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. We currently use:

  • Google Play Services & Apple App Store: For app distribution and basic platform analytics.
  • RevenueCat: To manage auto-renewing subscriptions and in-app purchases safely. (Note: We do not collect or store your credit card information; this is handled entirely by Apple and Google).
  • Google AdMob: To serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements within the app.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (by Google): To monitor app stability, track crashes, and understand how users interact with our menus and trivia questions.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our app and our third-party partners (like AdMob) use tracking technologies, such as mobile advertising IDs, to serve relevant ads. Depending on your region (such as the EU/EEA), you will be presented with a consent banner upon opening the app for the first time, allowing you to opt-in or opt-out of personalized advertising. You can also reset or disable your device's advertising ID at any time through your iOS or Android device settings.

5. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as is necessary to provide you with the services of Know Ball or to comply with legal obligations.

  • Account Deletion: If you have created an account, you have the right to delete it and all associated data at any time. You can request complete account deletion directly within the app by navigating to More > Delete Account, or by contacting us via email at the address provided below. Upon request, your account and gameplay data will be permanently erased from our active databases.

6. Children's Privacy

Know Ball is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions, including the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to securely delete that information.

7. Your GDPR Data Protection Rights

Since we are based in Romania, users within the European Economic Area (EEA) have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data.
  • Rectification: Request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Erasure: Request that we erase your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Request that we transfer the data we have collected to another organization, or directly to you.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We advise you to review this page periodically.
